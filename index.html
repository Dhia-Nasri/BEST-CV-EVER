Mechanical Design

Mechanical design is a vital aspect of engineering, requiring expertise in mechanical principles, materials, and manufacturing processes. A skilled mechanical designer utilizes specialized software and tools to create complex machinery and components, troubleshoots technical issues, and collaborates closely with other professionals to ensure the final product is safe, efficient, and meets client requirements. Mechanical design demands technical proficiency, creativity, and attention to detail to develop innovative and high-quality products.

CNC programming

CNC programming is the process of creating instructions for a CNC machine to produce a precise product. G-code is the standardized language used for writing these instructions. Popular CNC programming tools include Catia V5, Mastercam, Fusion 360, Vericut, and CGTech. CNC programming is critical for consistent and accurate production in manufacturing and other industries

It automates the production process, increasing efficiency and reducing errors. CNC machines produce high-quality products with a high level of accuracy and repeatability.
